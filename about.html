About me

I currently live in Vancouver, Canada and am studying Interactive Art and Technology at Simon Fraser University. Expected graduation: Fall/Winter 2023

I design digital products that focus on providing value through exceptional user experience. I add value to organizations and teams through facilitating conversations around strategies for user research, prototyping, copywriting, and testing. By identifying an appropriate strategy, I help create products that align user needs with business goals.
